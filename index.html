Australian Car Crash Analysis Report

Between 2010 and 2021, there were more than 14,000 crashes in Australia. The state with the most crashes during this period was New South Wales. There were 4,081 crashes in total. The area with the fewest crashes was the Australian Capital Territory, with 112 crashes. At the same time, the number of car accidents is showing a downward trend, which also means that Australian roads are safer.
